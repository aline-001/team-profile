Team Goals for Embedded Systems and IoT Project

1.Establish a seamless team workflow: We aim to combine our individual parts into one working system 
  where everyone’s contributions fit together perfectly.

2.Maintain an organized project history: Our goal is to keep our work tidy by writing clear, simple notes 
  for every update so we can easily track our journey from start to finish.

3.Create a user-friendly guide: We want to make sure our project is easy for anyone to understand by 
  writing a clear description of how our smart device works.

4.Build a reliable and functional solution: We are committed to working together to ensure our final product is stable, reliable, 
  and successfully solves the problem we set out to address.
